Buses

Register a bus, then assign it one or more routes — the driver/conductor picks which is active from their phone, locally. Live status updates while the bus's Hub is online.

Routes

A route is a stop list any number of buses can share. Find or create a stop here — manage its audio and ads in Content > Stop Names.

Online buses update instantly; offline buses catch up when they reconnect.

Content Library

Organized the way the audio actually works: common announcement clips, per-stop names, and the two ad formats.

Every stop: attention please → next stop is → stop name. The last stop of a trip also plays thanks for the journey. Uploading replaces the current clip for that slot.

Every stop, from every route, in one place — a stop added while building a route shows up here automatically. Upload the plain name clip, optionally an ad-combined clip, and toggle which one plays.

Image ads shown on the passenger display — either as a small bottom banner, or fullscreen (center, like a video ad). Each rotates on its own display duration.

Full-screen video or image ads that take over the passenger display center area. Images show for the duration you set, then return to stop info.

Ad Campaigns

Budgets and billing for ads tied to a campaign. Content with no campaign (PSA/house ads) always plays for free, unaffected by anything here.

Play-Count Analytics

How many times each announcement/ad has played, fleet-wide, based on plays reported up from every bus.

Hub Software Updates

Upload a new Hub release, then Publish it when you're ready to roll out. Buses check for the latest published release on their own, download it in the background, and switch over the next time they're idle (no active trip) — never mid-route. See hub/README.md for how to cut a release with scripts/build-release.js.
